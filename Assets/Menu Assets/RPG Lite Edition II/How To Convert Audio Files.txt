How To Convert/Downsample Audio Files:

---

1. Download Audacity at https://www.audacityteam.org/download/

2. Open up Audacity and load the audio file of your choosing

3. On the main button tabs near the top left corner, select "File", and then select "Export Audio"

4. Directly under the File name, you will see the types of sampling you can choose. Press the button to the right of "Save as type:"
	to view a drop-down menu of different audio conversions. 

5. Select a type of audio conversion (Note: Selecting "Options..." under the "Save" and "Cancel" buttons will allow you to further
	customize your type of audio conversion).

6. Press "Save" to bring up the "Edit Metadata" menu. Leave everything unchanged and select "OK" at the bottom.
